Account Deletion Request

We respect your privacy and provide explicit controls to delete your account, wipe your personal information, and clear your shared history at any time.

To delete your account and wipe all associated data, please use the "Delete Account" button inside the app settings, or email us directly at sny.apps77@gmail.com with your registered email address. Requests are processed within 24 hours.

What gets deleted when a request is processed?

  • Your primary user profile data and authentication identifiers are wiped entirely.
  • You are systematically unlinked and removed from every shared space/friend group you belong to.
  • All image files and media footprints uploaded by your account are permanently erased from our cloud servers.
