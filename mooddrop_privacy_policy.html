User-Generated Content & Shared Images (Crucial Disclosure)

Our Application allows users to upload, transmit, and share images within closed, private groups of friends (maximum of 5-6 people per group). While using the Application, we may collect and process:

  • Pictures and metadata (including EXIF data, where not stripped by your device) from your Device's camera and photo library.
